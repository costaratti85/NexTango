# Structural Destination

SistemaIndustrial must grow toward three real interfaces:

1. Seller workstation for internal order/cotation capture.
2. Customer totem for assisted self-service at the company.
3. Customer portal for external self-service after security review.

These are not immediate MVP scope, but they are architectural destinations.
The system must keep presets, pricing, quotation logic and traceability reusable across the three interfaces.
